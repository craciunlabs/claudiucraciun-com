What this is

I have been working with traditional Amazonian plant medicine since 2018. Over eighteen ceremonies with the same indigenous family in Peru — not a festival, not a Westernised retreat, not a package tour. The real thing.

I go with you. From the planning call to the flight, through the retreat, until you are home and integrated. You are never navigating this alone. The medicine is held by the local family — that is their work and their tradition. My role is to prepare you properly, be present with you throughout, and make sure you come home with clarity rather than confusion.

Integration is where most of the real work happens. The twelve months after matter as much as the time in Peru. That is why the support does not end when the plane lands.
